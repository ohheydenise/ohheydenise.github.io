Design Principles

Rule of Thirds

Family Search

https://www.familysearch.org/en/

Family Search makes good use of the Rule of Thirds by placing the introductory content and 'call to action' on the left side of the screen - where the human eye is most likely to look first.

Proximity

Etsy

https://www.etsy.com/

Etsy is a great example of how to use proximity in a proper way. All the different sections are well-defined and clearly labeled, letting you know where to look for certain things.

Contrast

V76 by Vaughn

https://www.v76.com

V76 by Vaughn has wonderful contrasting elements on its main page. There is a clear visual heirarchy between the four main elements by having the more bold element to the left and a lot of smaller elements to the right.
