Target Audience

The audience we are aiming for is really anybody who is interested in the weather. It could range from maybe a grade school student to someone in their 90s. Anybody can use our service.

Personas

Rebecca

A commercial airline pilot who needs to pay attention to the weather in different areas along her routes.

Bradley

A farmer who relies on weather to determine how the next crop season might unfold so he can start preparing sooner.

Scenarios

  1. A family is traveling to the mountains in late January to go skiing, as well as camping. Checking the weather is important so they can be prepared for driving on those roads.
  2. A man lives in a largely-populated metropolitan area and commutes to the big city every day. Depending on the weather, the traffic ranges from bad to terrible. He will need to know when to leave home.
  3. A group of thrill seekers wants to go skydiving. The weather will definitely have an affect on how their overall experience goes. Therefore, they would want to check it.
  4. A windsurfer needs to practice for an upcoming local tournament. In order to have the most effective practice, she will want to check and see what the weather will be like.
  5. A newly-wedded couple is going to take a road trip across the country for their honeymoon. They'll want to check if there will be any potential hazards along the way, weather-wise.
